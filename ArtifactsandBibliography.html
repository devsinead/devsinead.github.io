Rafferty, Sinead. (2019, June 6th, Spring 2019). Recreating Hiroshima Sugimoto. BIS 323 History of Photography, Professor Caplow

Photo Series PPTX

A series of photos presented in a formal essay and presentation analyzing Sugimoto's art and Sinead's own recreation of his art. The recreation photos were made with inspiration from Sugimoto using a standard DSLR camera and Adobe Lightroom photo editing software.

Rafferty, S.(2019, March 13th, Winter 2019). The World of Yayoi Kusama. BIS 471 Women in Art, Professor Caplow

Word Docx

Essay analyzing the impact of the work of Yayoi Kusama a Japanese artist now distinguished for their unique form of art style and the social impact they created in the art world during the late 20th century. The essay goes into depth analyzing Kusama's personal life and how it connects back to how it influenced their career in the art world.

Word Docx
